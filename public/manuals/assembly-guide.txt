========================================================================
                      BRICK & THROTTLE ASSEMBLY MANUAL
========================================================================
Congratulations on purchasing your premium high-performance racing kit!

This kit includes the full standard block assembly set PLUS the 
integrated high-speed RC (Remote Control) motorization upgrade.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1: UNBOXING & INVENTORY
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Verify all parts bags (Numbered 1-12) are present.
2. Check the RC Power kit box, which includes:
   - 1x Rechargeable Lithium Smart Battery Box
   - 1x 2.4GHz Wireless Remote Controller
   - 2x High-Torque L-Motors (for driving)
   - 1x Servo Motor (for steering)
   - 1x USB Charging Cable
3. Prepare a clean, wide workspace with containers to separate pins 
   and gears.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: CHASSIS & RC INTEGRATION
------------------------------------------------------------------------
*IMPORTANT: The RC motors must be built directly INTO the chassis during 
Steps 12-45. Do not finish the model before inserting the motors.*

1. Begin build with the central differential axle.
2. Mount the steering Servo Motor in the front compartment, linking 
   the gear drive to the steering rack.
3. Place the dual L-Motors in the rear engine bay, connecting them to 
   the rear differential gear system.
4. Route power cables along the guide channels toward the center of 
   the chassis.
5. Install the Smart Battery Box in the cockpit area where it will be 
   protected yet accessible for charging.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: PAIRING & TESTING
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Before mounting the body panels, test the electronics:
1. Turn on the Smart Battery Box (green indicator light turns on).
2. Switch on the 2.4GHz remote controller.
3. Test steering (left/right on left joystick).
4. Test drive (forward/backward on right joystick).
5. If movement is reversed, flip the toggle switch on the battery box.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4: BODY PANELS & RACING AESTHETICS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Build and snap-fit the side panels and doors.
2. Attach the front splitter and hood cover.
3. Complete the build with the rear wing assembly.
4. Apply decals precisely using tweezers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Build. Race. Repeat.
